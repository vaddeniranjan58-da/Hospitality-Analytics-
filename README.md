# Hospitality Analytics – Revenue & Booking Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

The Hospitality Analytics project focuses on analyzing hotel booking and revenue data to uncover business insights that help improve occupancy, reduce cancellations, and maximize revenue.

Using SQL, Power BI, and data analytics techniques, this project transforms raw hospitality data into meaningful visualizations and actionable recommendations for hotel management.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

The hospitality industry generates large volumes of booking, revenue, occupancy, and customer data every day. Hotel management often struggles to identify booking trends, understand cancellation behavior, evaluate customer satisfaction, and optimize revenue.

This project aims to analyze hospitality data and provide data-driven insights that support strategic decision-making and operational efficiency.

- Total Revenue
- Occupancy Percentage
- Average Rating
- Total Bookings
- Cancellation Rate
- Revenue Realization Percentage
- ADR (Average Daily Rate)
- RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room)

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Identified hotels with the highest revenue generation.
- Analyzed room categories contributing most to revenue.
- Evaluated cancellation trends and their impact on profitability.
- Measured customer satisfaction using ratings data.
- Identified opportunities to improve occupancy and revenue realization.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Reduce cancellations through flexible pricing and promotional offers.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing room categories.
- Improve customer experience to increase ratings and repeat bookings.
- Monitor occupancy trends to optimize pricing strategies.
- Implement data-driven decision-making for operational efficiency.
